## 📘 Key Traffic Laws

Driving laws are structured to protect everyone on the road — drivers, passengers, pedestrians, and cyclists alike. These laws govern everything from **speed limits** to **right of way** and **mandatory safety equipment**. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

---

## 🚗 Speed Limits (With Explanation)

Speed limits are strict and vary based on road type:

🛣️ **Highways (Autostrăzi)** — up to **130 km/h**  
🚙 **Expressways & country roads** — up to **100 km/h**  
🚗 **Outside towns** — up to **90 km/h**  
🏘️ **Inside towns and cities** — up to **50 km/h**  

🔎 Always follow posted signs — they can override the general limits!:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}

## 📜 Fundamental Rules Every Driver Must Know

### 🛑 Drive on the RIGHT  
Traffic moves on the right — overtaking is done on the left.:contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

### 🚸 Priority & Crosswalks  
Pedestrians have priority at crosswalks. Failure to stop can lead to fines and points.:contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}

### 🚨 Obey Traffic Officers  
If a police officer signals you to stop, you must comply immediately — ignoring orders can lead to serious penalties.:contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}

## 🚨 Zero Tolerance for Alcohol  

Romania follows a zero‑tolerance policy for alcohol in drivers — meaning **even a very low blood alcohol content** can lead to serious fines and suspensions.:contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}

---

## 📋 Mobile Phones and Safety Devices

📵 **Using a phone while driving is illegal** unless on hands‑free mode. Failure to comply is punishable by fines and points.:contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}

💡 It’s also strongly recommended to always wear headlights when driving outside localities, even in daylight — this improves visibility.:contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}

## 🧠 Why You Must Know These Laws

Understanding these laws keeps you safe and helps you avoid expensive penalties or license loss. Always review local rules and posted signage before driving. :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}
