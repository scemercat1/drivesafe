## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### 🚗 How high can fines go for speeding?
Fines for speeding depend on how far over the limit you go:  
- 10–20 km/h → modest fine + points  
- 21–30 km/h → larger fine + more points  
- 31–40 km/h → even larger  
- 41–50 km/h → major fine + many points  
- More than 50 km/h → high fine **and likely license suspension** for 90 days:contentReference[oaicite:21]{index=21}

---

### 🧑‍⚖️ What happens if I accumulate many penalty points?
If you accumulate **15 points within a year**, your license can be suspended by authorities. It’s designed to remove habitual risky drivers.:contentReference[oaicite:22]{index=22}

---

### 📵 Can I use my phone while driving?
Using a handheld phone is illegal — you must use hands‑free if you need to call or navigate.:contentReference[oaicite:23]{index=23}

---

### 🚫 Is drunk driving allowed at low levels?
Romania has a **zero‑tolerance alcohol policy** — even low alcohol levels can lead to penalties and suspension.:contentReference[oaicite:24]{index=24}
